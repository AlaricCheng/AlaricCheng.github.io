I'm a Research Fellow at the [Centre for Quantum Technologies (CQT), National University of Singapore](https://www.cqt.sg/), collaborating with Prof. [Patrick Rebentrost](https://www.cqt.sg/people/patrick-rebentrost/).


I got my PhD degree in February 2024 from University of Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. [Zhengfeng Ji](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/info/1312/4388.htm) and Prof. [Michael Bremner](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/Michael.Bremner).  Before my Ph.D. journey, I earned my B.Sc. in Physics from Southern University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. [Man-Hong Yung](https://yung.phy.sustech.edu.cn/). My research interest lies in the theory of quantum computing, with a particular focus on quantum advantage, quantum algorithms, verification and quantum circuit analysis.
